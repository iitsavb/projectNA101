Most asked questions about North Africa

What countries does North Africa consist of?

North Africa consists of 5 countries: Morocco, Algeria,Tunisia, Libya and Egypt.
The first three countries are also referred to as the Maghreb since they used to be one country all together.
The people of North Africa are predominantly Muslim and their official languages are arabic and berber.

What race are North Africans?

So in North Africa there are two types of people:The North Africans and the Afro-Arabs. The original
North African inhabitants are North African but due to colonisation of the Arabs in 645 CE, the population has become more diverse.
So it is false to claim that all the people are Arab or North African since most Afro-arabs have some North African in them.

Who invented couscous?

Couscous was invented before Morocco, Algeria and Tunisia split up in their own countries.
It was invented as early as 238 bce indicating that all three of them invented it.
